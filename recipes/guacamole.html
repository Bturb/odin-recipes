Guacamole

This party snack favourite is simple, nutritious and delicious. Perfect to serve alongside corn chips or bread. We add a good amount of lime juice to add some great depth of flavour, as well as keeping your Guac fresher for longer!

Prep Time: 25mins Servings: 20

Ingredients:

  • 5 avocados, peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 2tbls fresh lime juice
  • 3/4cup minced green onion
  • 1/2cup minced coriander
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice in a bowl.
  2. Add the green onion and coriander and mix well.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately or cover and store in fridge (hint: store with avocado pits to keep from browning.
